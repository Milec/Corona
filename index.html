II

The Power

A Solarian draws power from the light of stars. Marcus is a Blue Sun — the rarest and most extreme category ever theorized, and the only confirmed genuine Solarian of the modern era.

The Solar Spectrum

A Solarian's color is fixed at birth and follows the lifecycle of stars — from coolest to hottest, youngest to most extreme. It sets the ceiling of their power.

Attunement

In play, a Solarian shifts between two opposing states. Click to attune.

Stellar Stages & the Helian Lifespan

Among Helians, advancement follows a bell curve. Early stages extend life far beyond the baseline; past the peak of Main Sequence — Prime, each stage burns through the body to sustain greater output. A level-20 Solarian may be 1,400 years old with only decades left.

III

The World

The Helios Hegemony — a solar civilization built on selective breeding, martial hierarchy, and an obsession with the number seven. Once it ruled a star system. Now it is a conquered vassal, quietly hollowed out from within.

The Solar Throne

The Rule of Seven

Seven continents. Seven houses. Seven months. Seven children per dynasty per cycle. Forty-nine children born each succession. Whether tradition or deliberate terraforming, no scholar can say.
