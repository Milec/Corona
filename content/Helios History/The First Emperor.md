## Overview

Before [[Marcus]], there was one other.

The first Hegemon of the [[Helios Hegemony]] — the warrior-king from whom the [[Caerulius Bloodline]] takes its name — was the first and only confirmed **Blue Sun** in all of Helian history. Every throne, every house, every ritual of the civilization descends from what he built. And for all of it, no one remembers where he came from.

---

## The Blue Sun Sovereign

Ancient records describe a being of unmatched [[Solarian]] power: a Blue Sun in an age that had never seen one and would not see one again. Through conquest, diplomacy, and sheer force of will, he united the seven continents of [[Helios Prime]] beneath a single banner and forged the foundation of the Hegemony.

To keep the great powers from fracturing after his death, he bound [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]] to the throne through blood, establishing the tradition that became the [[Imperial Offering]]. It is from the color of his corona — a blue sun — that the imperial line takes both its name and its mandate.

---

## The Erased Origin

Whether lost to time or deliberately removed, the emperor's family of origin is unknown. No dynasty can claim descent from him; no house can say the first Blue Sun was theirs. That absence is not an accident of history so much as its load-bearing wall — because no house owns the imperial line, the throne has stood above all seven for centuries. The mystery is the mandate.

Among the people of the Hegemony, he is remembered with near-religious reverence: not merely the first ruler of their kind, but a divinely favored being whose blood carries the right to rule. Few openly question the legend, because the legitimacy of the entire Hegemony rests upon believing it.

---

## The Second Blue Sun

Here is the wound the Hegemony has never been able to close. For millennia its bloodline programs chased a second Blue Sun and never caught one — the [[Helion Nodes]] that make such a being possible cannot be engineered, only bred toward and hoped for. The nobility built an entire civilization on the promise of the first, and produced only imitations of him.

Then a child was born to the last house of the current cycle, exiled in secret before the court could learn what he was.

The first Blue Sun built the order that rules [[Helios Prime]]. What the second does with the same fire has not yet been written — and there is no one left alive who knows what it means for a second sun to rise over a throne that was only ever meant to have one.
