## Overview

Helion Nodes are unique biological structures capable of absorbing and processing ambient stellar radiation, and the source of the [[Helian Race]]'s heightened abilities. Rather than creating true uncontrolled fusion, they catalyze microscopic fusion reactions within highly regulated intracellular environments, converting stellar energy into biological output with extreme efficiency.

The nodes are also responsible for the race's extraordinary affinity for [[Solarian]] power. When your own body already embodies the principles of a star, it is far easier to take to its abilities.

---

## The Limit of Genecraft

Though Helian genecraft has mastered the manipulation of conventional traits — intelligence, reflexes, temperament, disease resistance, and dynasty-specific aptitudes — Helion Nodes remain beyond true artificial control.

[[The Seven Noble Dynasties]] regularly engineer traits aligned with their roles. [[House Bellator]] cultivates superior combat instincts and physical aptitude, while [[House Logos]] enhances cognitive capability and pattern recognition. Such modifications are routine throughout noble society.

Helion Nodes, however, do not function according to simple genetic rules.

---

## The Fusion Lattice

Their efficiency appears to arise from a phenomenon known as the Fusion Lattice — a highly complex interaction of inherited genes, epigenetic markers, and lineage-specific traits built across generations. Attempts to engineer them directly have consistently resulted in instability and failure.

As a result, Helian science can refine bloodlines, but cannot create them. For this reason, the nobility turned to selective breeding, arranged unions, and lineage preservation.

In the [[Helios Hegemony]], blood itself became the final frontier of science.
