## Overview

The Imperial Offering is the ancient ritual through which [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]] bind themselves to the throne of the [[Caerulius Bloodline]] through blood.

Upon the ascension of a new Hegemon, each of the seven dynasties presents **seven daughters** to the Imperial Court:

- **One Principal Wife** — elevated as the dynasty's primary court representative, she joins the [[Matrilium]]
- **Six Concubines** — reinforce bonds between the throne and each house

Each woman bears one child in order of rank, producing **seven children per dynasty**. Across the full seven-month calendar — one month per dynasty — **49 children** are born in total per succession cycle.

---

## The Calendar

The Hegemony's calendar is divided into **seven months**, each corresponding to one of the seven noble families. The Hegemon spends one month with each family in turn, structuring the offering so that every house has a statistically equal chance of producing an heir.

---

## Naming Structure

All children produced through the Offering share the monarch's family name **Caerulius** alongside their mother's house name. Their cognomen reflects birth order by concubine rank — and this structure applies equally to **every house**:

| Rank | Cognomen | Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Main Wife | Prima / Primus | First |
| 1st Concubine | Secunda / Secundus | Second |
| 2nd Concubine | Tertia / Tertius | Third |
| 3rd Concubine | Quarta / Quartus | Fourth |
| 4th Concubine | Quinta / Quintus | Fifth |
| 5th Concubine | Sexta / Sextus | Sixth |
| 6th Concubine | Sept | Seven |

> *Sept is not unique to House Bellator. Every house produces a Sept — the child of the sixth concubine — and every house produces a Prima. The cognomen indicates rank within the Offering, not the house's position in the cycle.*

---

## Significance of Seven

The repetition of the number seven throughout Helian culture — seven continents, seven months, seven dynasties, seven children per house — is pervasive enough that scholars debate whether it is organic tradition or the result of **deliberate terraforming and societal engineering**. The truth remains unknown.
