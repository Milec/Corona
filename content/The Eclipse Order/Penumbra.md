## Overview

Penumbra is the name [[The Eclipse Knights]] give their home — a sprawling space habitat in the outer fringe beyond Imperial territory, built and grown across generations around a blue sun. For most of the order, it is the only world they have ever known.

The name is chosen with the same awareness as the order's own: the penumbra is the outer shadow of an eclipse, the edge of the dark where the corona still shows.

---

## A Home Around a Blue Sun

The founders were sent beyond Imperial reach with a mission, not a destination. Where they finally stopped, they built — and over centuries the habitat expanded into a self-sustaining society orbiting a blue sun, chosen or found for its likeness to the star of [[Helios Prime]].

That sun is no coincidence of engineering. The blue sun holds a near-sacred place in the order's culture: it is what they came from, and what they are trying to return to. [[Helian Race|Helians]] are still born under it, and [[Solarian]] power still manifests among them — though on a minuscule scale compared to the height of the [[Helios Hegemony]].

---

## Hidden, Not Safe

Penumbra survives on a cold balance. Under the [[The Eclipse Treaty]], the Empire monitors the order and the Hegemony pretends it does not exist; the order, for its part, never strikes at a scale that would make its destruction worth the cost. The habitat is known to exist and left alone — precisely because leaving it alone is cheaper than ending it.

That arithmetic holds only so long as the order stays what the Empire believes it to be. What Penumbra becomes if it ever fields a Solarian who changes the sum — a second Blue Sun, say, who does not stay in a cell — is a question the order's long-term doctrine has never had to answer.
