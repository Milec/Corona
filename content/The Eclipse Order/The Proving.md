## Overview

The Proving is the solo trial that turns a Squire of [[The Eclipse Knights]] into a Blade. There is no time limit and no assistance. There is only a target the order judges a true test of the Squire's current ability — and the question of whether the Squire comes back.

Aspirants surrender the right to a name when they join the order. The Proving is how they earn one.

---

## The Trial

A Squire seeking full membership is dispatched, alone, to locate, engage, and defeat a designated target. The nature of the target varies; what does not vary is the standard by which it is chosen and the fact that no help is coming.

- **Return having succeeded**, and the Squire is elevated to Blade and granted a name.
- **Return having failed**, and one further attempt may be earned after more training — but only once.
- **Do not return**, and the Squire is mourned and remembered.

The name a Blade earns is not decorative. It is chosen to reflect both the order's history and the knight's own nature, and it is the first thing about them the order will consider true.

---

## Marcus's Proving

[[Marcus]] was sent into Imperial space to locate and defeat his designated target. He succeeded — and for it earned the name **Corona**, a name that speaks to what he is and what he might become.

On his way out of Imperial territory, he was identified and apprehended. His is the rare Proving that was completed and undone in a single stroke: a Squire who became a Blade in the eyes of the order, and a prisoner in the eyes of the Empire, in the same passage home.

Whether his target was chosen to test him or to remove someone the order wanted gone — and whether a name, once earned, can ever be taken back — are not questions the order answers aloud.
