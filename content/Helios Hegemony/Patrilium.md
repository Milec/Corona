## Overview

The Patrilium is the supreme advisory council of the [[Helios Hegemony]], composed of the heads — known as Patriarchs — of each of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]]. Despite the title's masculine origin, membership is determined by the position of house head, not gender; female Patriarchs hold full and equal standing within the council.

The Patrilium's authority covers governance, military affairs, intercontinental policy, and all matters of state that fall beneath the absolute authority of the Hegemon. Neither the Patrilium nor the [[Matrilium]] may override the Hegemon — but wise rulers heed their counsel.

## Members

**Varro Caerulius Aegis Tertius** — [[House Aegis]]
Patriarch of the first house. Deliberate and economical, he speaks rarely but commands absolute attention when he does. Regarded by most as the council's strategic conscience.

**Cassia Caerulius Aurum Sept** — [[House Aurum]]
Patriarch of the second house. One of the council's female Patriarchs. Controls Sol Aurelia's energy distribution agreements and wields economic leverage with quiet authority. Has never needed to raise her voice to be obeyed.

**Orin Caerulius Genesis Quinta** — [[House Genesis]]
Patriarch of the third house. The quietest power on the council. Thinks in generations rather than political cycles, and is deeply aware that controlling the food supply and bloodline sciences is a more durable form of power than any fleet.

**Auren Caerulius Logos Prima** — [[House Logos]]
Patriarch of the fourth house. One of the council's female Patriarchs. The most credentialed member of the Patrilium and primary architect of the Hegemony's artificial intelligence doctrine. Asks the questions others did not think to ask.

**Davan Caerulius Veil Secunda** — [[House Veil]]
Patriarch of the fifth house. The most unsettling presence on the council — not for what he says, but for how little he says and how much he clearly knows. Governs Vel Astra from facilities that do not appear on public records.

**Elia Caerulius Vox Quarta** — [[House Vox]]
Patriarch of the sixth house. One of the council's female Patriarchs. The most publicly visible Patriarch in the Hegemony. Speaks on behalf of the Patrilium in all formal addresses and is fully aware that visibility is itself a form of power.

**Rael Caerulius Ensis Sexta** — [[House Bellator]]
Patriarch of the seventh house. Commands the Hegemony's expeditionary fleets and has personally led campaigns rather than directing them from orbit — an unusual distinction among sitting Patriarchs.

---

## Role & Authority

- Advises the Hegemon on governance, military affairs, and intercontinental policy
- Represents the interests of each dynasty at the imperial level
- Operates in parallel with the [[Matrilium]], which handles succession, diplomacy, and court influence
- Cannot overrule the Hegemon, but collectively represents a political force no ruler ignores without consequence
