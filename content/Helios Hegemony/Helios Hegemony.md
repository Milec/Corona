## Overview

The Helios Hegemony is the sovereign civilization of the [[Helian Race]], centered on [[Helios Prime]] in the [[Helios System]]. At its height, the Hegemony controlled its entire star system — maintaining colonies, fleets, and stations around every major celestial body. It was a civilization defined by selective breeding, solar religion, martial hierarchy, and an obsessive cultural relationship with the number seven.

Several hundred years ago, the Hegemony was conquered by the Algalterian Galactic Empire. It has existed as a vassal state ever since — nominally self-governing, functionally dependent, and quietly hollowed out from within.

---

## Structure

The Hegemony is ruled by a single monarch, the **Hegemon**, drawn from the [[Caerulius Bloodline]]. Beneath the throne sit [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], each governing one of the seven continents of Helios Prime and one pillar of imperial civilization.

The Hegemon rules with the counsel of two parallel advisory bodies:
- The **[[Patrilium]]** — composed of the seven Patriarchs, governing matters of state, military, and intercontinental policy
- The **[[Matrilium]]** — composed of the seven Principal Wives, governing succession, diplomacy, and court affairs

Neither council can overrule the Hegemon. Both are ignored at great cost.

The mechanism of succession is the **[[Imperial Offering]]** and the **[[Heir Candidacy System]]** — a structured competition among the 49 children born of each succession cycle, from which the next Hegemon is eventually appointed.

---

## The Blue Sun

[[Helios Prime]] orbits a **blue sun** — a fact that carries profound cultural and religious significance for the Helian people. The sun is not merely a star. It is the origin of [[Solarian]] power, the source of the [[Helion Nodes]] that define the Helian body, and the symbolic center of everything the civilization values: heat, light, dominance, and the capacity to burn away what is lesser.

The Hegemony's calendar, its architecture, its religion, and its system of governance all orbit the sun as surely as the planet does.

---

## The Significance of Seven

The number seven permeates every layer of Helian civilization: seven continents, seven noble houses, seven months, seven children per dynasty per cycle, seven star system colonies at the height of their expansion. Whether this is organic cultural tradition or the result of deliberate terraforming and societal engineering from some earlier era remains unknown — even to the Hegemony's own scholars.

---

## Current State

The Hegemony that exists today is a diminished and compromised version of what it once was. The Algalterian conquest stripped it of its star system territories, replaced its true ruling class with collaborators, and suppressed the Solarian abilities that had once defined its identity as a civilization.

The current nobility governs through a combination of genuine competence, inherited prestige, and technological imitation of powers they no longer truly possess. The population largely accepts the official history. The [[Matrilium]] and [[Patrilium]] continue to function, the [[Heir Candidacy System]] continues to produce successors, and the appearance of an ancient and sovereign civilization is carefully maintained.

Beneath it, the machinery of a conquered people turns — and has turned, for several hundred years, without anyone left who remembers how it was before.
