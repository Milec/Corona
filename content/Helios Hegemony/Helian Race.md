## Overview

The Helians are a race of warrior-like humanoids native to the [[Helios System]]. Though populations exist across the Hegemony's many worlds, they primarily reside upon [[Helios Prime]], where they constitute both the dominant population and the ruling caste of the [[Helios Hegemony]].

In outward appearance, Helians closely resemble baseline humans. However, they are distinguished by several defining traits: idealized physiques, monochromatic hair coloration, and unusually vibrant eyes displaying hues rarely found among other species.

Beneath these cosmetic differences lies a far more extraordinary distinction. Embedded within every Helian cell are specialized organelles known as [[Helion Nodes]].

---

## Cell Fusion

The process by which the nodes operate, commonly referred to as Cell Fusion, dramatically increases metabolic performance and cellular productivity. The result is a species possessing naturally elevated physical capabilities, superior endurance, heightened resilience, accelerated recovery, and physiques that appear genetically optimized.

In current recorded history, Cell Fusion is believed responsible only for Helian physical superiority. Forgotten records and deeper analysis, however, suggest something far more significant.

---

## The Solarian Affinity

The same [[Helion Nodes]] that empower the Helian body also appear to create an abnormally high affinity for [[Solarian]] manifestation.

While Solarian abilities are not innate and must still awaken naturally — a process long since forgotten and obscured — Helians exhibit substantially higher manifestation rates than other known races. The overwhelming majority of Helian Solarians manifested as Yellow Suns, with stronger manifestations historically appearing disproportionately among noble bloodlines.

The efficiency of an individual's Cell Fusion process varies significantly and appears strongly influenced by genetics. Minor differences in Helion Node density, energy conversion efficiency, and cellular stability can produce dramatic differences in performance.

---

## The Pursuit of the Blue Sun

This reality shaped much of Helian civilization and gave rise to millennia of selective breeding, bloodline engineering, and eugenic practices among the noble houses of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. It can be traced as the origin of the [[Imperial Offering]].

Officially, these efforts sought to preserve the strength and purity of noble lineages.

Unofficially, they pursued a singular ambition: the creation of a Helian whose Helion Nodes possessed such extraordinary efficiency that Solarian power would no longer require awakening at all.

Though no confirmed case has ever been documented, forgotten ancient theories suggest that a Helian with sufficiently evolved Helion Nodes could naturally sustain Solarian abilities from birth — manifesting not as a Yellow Sun, but as a Blue Sun.

---

## Baseline and Awakened

A baseline, unawakened Helian is comparable to an Olympic-level human athlete, with a natural lifespan reaching into the 150s.

An Awakened Helian cannot be reasonably quantified; the difference between the two is immensely vast.
