## Overview

The fourth planet of the [[Helios System]], cradle of the [[Helian Race]] and capital of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. Helios Prime is a massive, tidally locked super-Earth nearly three times the size of Earth. One hemisphere is scorched beneath perpetual daylight — endless deserts and solar-burned wastelands — while the opposite hemisphere lies frozen in eternal darkness beneath glaciers and sub-zero storms.

Above it all burns the blue sun: the origin of [[Solarian]] power, the source of the [[Helion Nodes]] that define the Helian body, and the symbolic center of everything the civilization holds sacred.

---

## The Terminus Belt

Between the planet's two extremes runs the Terminus Belt, a narrow habitable band circling the world along the prime meridian. Within it stand the seven great continents of the Hegemony, each governed by one of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]] and dedicated to a pillar of imperial civilization:

1. [[Aegyrium Continent]] — defensive command, logistics, and strategic coordination, under House Aegis
2. [[Aurion Continent]] — wealth, trade, and energy production, under House Aurum
3. [[Genethra Continent]] — agriculture, genetics, and biological sciences, under House Genesis
4. [[Logyra Continent]] — research, artificial intelligence, and technological advancement, under House Logos
5. [[Velkaris Continent]] — intelligence, espionage, and covert operations, under House Veil
6. [[Voxara Continent]] — governance, diplomacy, communications, and cultural influence, under House Vox
7. [[Bellaris Continent]] — offensive military power, expeditionary fleets, and martial aristocracy, under House Bellator

Together, the seven continents form the political and industrial backbone of the Hegemony, bound beneath the supreme authority of the [[Caerulius Bloodline]].
