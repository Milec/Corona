The fourth planet in the [[Helios System]], cradle of the [[Helian Race]] and capital of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. [[Helios Prime]] is a massive tidally locked super-Earth nearly three times the size of Earth. One hemisphere is scorched beneath perpetual daylight, consumed by endless deserts and solar-burned wastelands, while the opposite hemisphere lies frozen in eternal darkness beneath glaciers and sub-zero storms.

Between these extremes lies the Terminus Belt, a narrow habitable region circling the planet along the prime meridian. Within this band stand the seven great continents of the Hegemony, each governed by one of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]] and dedicated to a critical pillar of imperial civilization:

1. [[Aegyrium Continent]] – Defensive command, logistics, and strategic coordination under House Aegis
2. [[Aurion Continent]] – Wealth, trade, and energy production under House Aurum
3. [[Genethra Continent]] – Agriculture, genetics, and biological sciences under House Genesis
4. [[Logyra Continent]] – Research, artificial intelligence, and technological advancement under House Logos
5. [[Velkaris Continent]] – Intelligence, espionage, and covert operations under House Veil
6. [[Voxara Continent]] – Governance, diplomacy, communications, and cultural influence under House Vox
7. [[Bellaris Continent]] – Offensive military power, expeditionary fleets, and martial aristocracy under House Bellator

Together, the Seven Continents form the political and industrial backbone of the Hegemony, bound beneath the supreme authority of the [[Caerulius Bloodline]].
