**Current Hegemon:** 
*Aurion Caerulius Genesis Prima*

The [[Caerulius Bloodline]] descends from the first Helian emperor, a warrior-king said to possess unmatched Solarian power. Ancient records describe him as the first and only Blue Sun, a phenomenon never seen again in Helian history, from which the bloodline takes its name. Through conquest, diplomacy, and sheer force of will, he united the seven continents of [[Helios Prime]] beneath a single banner and forged the foundation of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. To prevent the great powers from fracturing after his death, he bound [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]] to the imperial throne through blood, creating the tradition known as the Imperial Offering.

Whether lost to time or deliberately erased, the emperor’s family of origin remains unknown. No dynasty can claim direct ownership of the imperial line, a mystery that has helped preserve the throne’s authority above the seven houses for centuries.

The Imperial Offering is the foundation of the Hegemony’s dynastic order. Upon the ascension of a new Hegemon, each of the seven noble houses sends seven women of their bloodlines to the imperial court. From among them, one is chosen as the Imperial Consort while the remaining six serve as royal concubines. Each woman is permitted to bear only a single child, ensuring that every generation of the imperial line carries the blood of all seven dynasties while preventing any one house from dominating succession through sheer numbers.  

Succession within the imperial family is governed through the [[Heir Candidacy System]], a process designed to ensure that only the most capable heir may ascend to the Solar Throne.  

Among the people of the [[Helios Hegemony]], the Caerulius Bloodline is regarded with near-religious reverence. To many, the Blue Sun Emperor was not merely the first ruler of humanity, but a divinely favored being whose blood carries the mandate to rule. Though some scholars question the literal truth of the Blue Sun legend, few openly challenge it, as the legitimacy of the entire Hegemony rests upon the belief that the Caerulius line stands above all other dynasties.
