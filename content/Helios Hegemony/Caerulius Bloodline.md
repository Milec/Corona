## Overview

The Caerulius Bloodline is the imperial line of the [[Helios Hegemony]] — the dynasty of the Solar Throne, from which every Hegemon is drawn. It stands above [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], and its authority supersedes every law, title, and office in the realm.

**Current Hegemon:** [[The Hegemon|Aurion Caerulius Genesis Prima]]

---

## Descent from the First Blue Sun

The line descends from [[The First Emperor|the first Helian emperor]] — the warrior-king and first confirmed Blue Sun, who united the seven continents of [[Helios Prime]] and forged the Hegemony. It is from the color of his corona that the bloodline takes its name, and from his legend that it takes its claim to rule.

Because no dynasty can claim descent from him, none can claim the throne is theirs. That erased origin is not a gap in the record so much as the foundation of the line's authority: a throne no house owns is a throne that stands above all seven.

---

## The Mandate

Among the people of the Hegemony, the Caerulius line is regarded with near-religious reverence. To many, the Blue Sun Emperor was not merely the first ruler of their kind but a divinely favored being whose blood carries the right to rule. Some scholars quietly question the literal truth of the Blue Sun legend; few challenge it openly, because the legitimacy of the entire Hegemony rests on believing it.

What almost none of them know is that the mandate was real — and that the modern throne wields only a manufactured echo of the power that first earned it.

---

## Blood Bound to the Throne

To keep the great houses from fracturing after his death, the first emperor bound them to the throne through blood — the tradition that became the [[Imperial Offering]]. Upon each Hegemon's ascension, every dynasty sends seven women to the imperial court; each bears a single child, so that every generation of the line carries the blood of all seven houses and no house can dominate succession through numbers alone. From the children of each cycle, the [[Heir Candidacy System]] raises up the next Hegemon.
