A radiant continent of solar deserts, golden arcologies, and immense trade networks. Aurion controls the Hegemony's wealth, energy production, and intercontinental commerce. Noble dynasties here wield influence through finance, political patronage, and economic leverage, turning prosperity itself into a weapon of power.
