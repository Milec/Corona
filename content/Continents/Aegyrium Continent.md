The first continent of [[Helios Prime]]

![[9B22AEE5-4114-40FC-B524-E63842FC2ACE.png]]

The heavily fortified heartland of the Hegemony, Aegyrium specializes in defense, logistics, and strategic command. Endless fortress-cities, orbital shield arrays, and military command nexuses dominate the continent. Its nobles are famed as tacticians, admirals, and guardians of imperial stability, maintaining the defensive systems that protect the world from both internal rebellion and external invasion.

**Ruling Family**: [[House Aegis]]
**Capital Megacity**: Aegyrion Prime
