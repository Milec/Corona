An unusually rich star system of dozens of worlds — once thriving Hegemony colonies, now largely desolate or repurposed as Algalterian stations. At its heart lies [[Helios Prime]], cradle of the [[Helian Race]] and seat of the [[Helios Hegemony]], orbiting the blue sun from which the entire civilization draws its identity and its power.

At the height of the Hegemony, the system was held in its entirety — fleets, colonies, and installations around every major body. The [[Helios History/Imperial Conquest|Imperial Conquest]] stripped all of it away. Under [[The Eclipse Treaty]], everything beyond Helios Prime and its immediate orbit passed into the hands of the [[Algalterian Galactic Empire]], and the outer system that Helians once built now belongs to the power that conquered them.
