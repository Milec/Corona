The fourth continent of [[Helios Prime]]

![[IMG_3604.png]]

The intellectual and technological core of the Hegemony, Logyra is home to colossal archives, research citadels, and machine-governed academies. Scientific innovation, artificial intelligence, and higher learning define the continent. Its nobles act as philosopher-engineers and scientific overseers, shaping the empire through knowledge and technological supremacy.  

**Ruling Family**: [[House Logos]]  
**Capital Megacity**: Logyrium Ascendant
