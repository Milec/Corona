The seventh continent of [[Helios Prime]]

A continent of grand martial estates, alpine citadels, and prestigious military academies. Bellaris serves as the offensive spearhead of the Hegemony, producing elite commanders, knightly orders, and expeditionary fleets. Its aristocracy values honor, conquest, and military excellence, with noble houses competing for prestige through campaigns and strategic victories.  

**Ruling Family**: [[House Bellator]]  
**Capital Megacity**: Bellator Rex
