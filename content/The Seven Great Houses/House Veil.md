## Overview

House Veil is the fifth of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], governing [[Velkaris Continent]] and operating as the shadow behind the Hegemony's throne. Where other houses command armies or control economies, Veil controls *information* — and in the Hegemony, information is the most dangerous resource of all.

**House Name (family name used by children):** Veil

---

## Role & Domain

- **Continental Seat:** [[Velkaris Continent]]
- **Capital Megacity:** Vel Astra
- **Pillar:** Intelligence, espionage, and covert operations
- **Genetic Specialization:** House Veil cultivates heightened perceptual acuity, emotional regulation, and deceptive capability in its bloodline — producing nobles who are difficult to read, impossible to surprise, and deeply comfortable operating in spaces where nothing is as it appears

---

## House Head (Patrilium Representative)

**Davan Caerulius Veil Secunda**
Patriarch of House Veil. A former heir candidate, his cognomen *Secunda* marks him as the child of a first concubine. He is the most unsettling presence on the [[Patrilium]] — not because he says threatening things, but because he says so little, watches so much, and is widely understood to know secrets about every other member of the council. He governs Vel Astra from an administrative complex that does not appear on any public record.

---

## Principal Wife (Matrilium Representative)

**Mira Veil**
Principal Wife of House Veil to the current Hegemon and sitting member of the [[Matrilium]]. She is universally liked — warm, attentive, and seemingly open. The other wives are aware this is deliberate. She is the council's foremost practitioner of social intelligence and has never once been caught in a lie, which the more perceptive members find more alarming than reassuring.
