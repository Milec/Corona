## Overview

House Logos is the fourth of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], governing [[Logyra Continent]] and serving as the intellectual engine of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. Its nobles are philosopher-engineers and scientific overseers who believe that knowledge precedes all other forms of power — and who have spent millennia proving it.

**House Name (family name used by children):** Logos

---

## Role & Domain

- **Continental Seat:** [[Logyra Continent]]
- **Capital Megacity:** Logyrium Ascendant
- **Pillar:** Research, artificial intelligence, and technological advancement
- **Genetic Specialization:** House Logos enhances cognitive capability and pattern recognition in its bloodline — producing nobles of exceptional analytical ability who process complex systems intuitively and who regard any unsolved problem as a personal affront

---

## House Head (Patrilium Representative)

**Auren Caerulius Logos Prima**
Patriarch of House Logos. A former heir candidate, her cognomen *Prima* marks her as the child of a principal wife — the highest birth rank in any house's cycle. She holds more advanced academic credentials than any other sitting Patriarch and governs Logyrium Ascendant's research agenda with the authority of someone who understands every project under her supervision in full. She is the primary architect of the Hegemony's artificial intelligence doctrine and the most likely Patriarch to ask a question no one else thought to ask.

---

## Principal Wife (Matrilium Representative)

**Ira Logos**
Principal Wife of House Logos to the current Hegemon and sitting member of the [[Matrilium]]. Meticulous, precise, and almost constitutionally incapable of suffering imprecision in others. Her contributions to the Matrilium tend to be comprehensive written analyses submitted before meetings begin — she considers open-floor debate an inefficient format.
