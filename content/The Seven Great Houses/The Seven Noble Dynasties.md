## Overview

The Seven Noble Dynasties are the great houses of the [[Helios Hegemony]] — the highest echelon beneath the Solar Throne. Ancient and immensely powerful in their own right, all seven stand in absolute subservience to the Hegemon of the [[Caerulius Bloodline]], whose authority supersedes every law, title, and office throughout the realm.

Each dynasty governs one of the seven continental domains of [[Helios Prime]], rules its planetary megacity-capital, and stewards one foundational pillar of civilization — a specialization refined across millennia of selective breeding.

## Bound Through Blood

Upon the ascension of a new Hegemon, each dynasty takes part in the [[Imperial Offering]] — presenting seven daughters to the imperial court: one Principal Wife, elevated as the dynasty's representative within the household, and six concubines. Through the ritual, the Hegemon's lineage becomes intertwined with all seven houses, ensuring their loyalty through blood as much as law.

---

## The Two Councils

The heads of the dynasties — the Patriarchs — form the [[Patrilium]], the supreme advisory council on governance, military affairs, and matters of state. Their Principal Wives compose the [[Matrilium]], whose influence runs through succession, diplomacy, social doctrine, and the hidden currents of court politics.

Neither council may overrule the Hegemon. Both exist to guide the throne, and wise rulers heed their counsel.
