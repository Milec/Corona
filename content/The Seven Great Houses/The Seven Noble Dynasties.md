The Seven Noble Dynasties form the highest echelon beneath the throne of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. Though ancient and immensely powerful in their own right, all stand in absolute subservience to the Hegemon of the [[Caerulius Bloodline]], whose authority supersedes every law, title, and office throughout the realm.

Each dynasty governs one of the seven continental domains of [[Helios Prime]] and rules its corresponding planetary megacity-capital. Across millennia, these houses evolved into specialized stewards of the Hegemony, with each dynasty assuming responsibility for a foundational pillar of civilization.

Upon the ascension of a new Hegemon, each dynasty participates in the ancient rite of Imperial Offering. Every house presents seven daughters to the Imperial Court:

- One Principal Wife, elevated as the dynasty’s primary representative within the imperial household
- Six Concubines, intended to reinforce bonds between throne and house

Through this ritual, the Hegemon’s lineage becomes intertwined with all seven dynasties, ensuring loyalty through blood as much as law.

The heads of the dynasties, known as Patriarchs, collectively form the [[Patrilium]], a supreme advisory body responsible for governance, military concerns, intercontinental policy, and matters of state.

The Principal Wives of the dynasties compose the [[Matrilium]], an equally prestigious council whose influence extends through succession, diplomacy, social doctrine, and the hidden currents of court politics.

Though neither council possesses authority above the Hegemon, both exist to guide the throne. Wise rulers heed their counsel.

Together the Seven Dynasties govern the machinery of civilization beneath the radiance of Helios itself.

The Seven Dynasties:

- [[House Bellator]] – War and Expansion

- [[House Aegis]] – Defense and Security

- [[House Veil]] – Intelligence and Espionage

- [[House Logos]] – Science and Knowledge

- [[House Vox]] – Diplomacy and Statecraft

- [[House Aurum]] – Stewardship and Industry

- [[House Genesis]] – Agriculture and Helian Sustenance
