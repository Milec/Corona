## Overview

House Bellator is the seventh of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], governing [[Bellaris Continent]] and serving as the offensive spearhead of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. Where [[House Aegis]] holds the line, Bellator drives it forward — producing elite commanders, knightly orders, and expeditionary fleets from a continent of grand martial estates, alpine citadels, and prestigious military academies.

**House Name (family name used by children):** Ensis

---

## Role & Domain

- **Continental Seat:** [[Bellaris Continent]]
- **Capital Megacity:** Bellator Rex
- **Pillar:** Offensive military power, expeditionary fleets, and martial aristocracy
- **Genetic Specialization:** House Bellator cultivates superior combat instincts, physical aptitude, and tactical aggression in its bloodline — producing nobles whose aristocracy is measured in campaigns won and enemies broken, and who regard the absence of conflict as a period of preparation

---

## House Head (Patrilium Representative)

**Rael Caerulius Ensis Sexta**
Patriarch of House Bellator. A former heir candidate, his cognomen *Sexta* marks him as the child of a fifth concubine. He governs Bellator Rex and commands the Hegemony's expeditionary fleets with the directness his house values above all other qualities. He has personally led campaigns rather than directing them from orbit — an unusual distinction among sitting Patriarchs, and one his rivals cannot easily dismiss.

---

## Principal Wife (Matrilium Representative)

**Draea Ensis**
Principal Wife of House Bellator to the current Hegemon and sitting member of the [[Matrilium]]. She is blunt in the manner particular to Bellator nobles — not unkind, but constitutionally uninterested in softening a point. She is the council's most reliable voice on matters of military readiness, frontier security, and the costs of war, and she has little patience for deliberations she considers theoretical.
