## Overview

House Vox is the sixth of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], governing [[Voxara Continent]] and holding authority over the voice of the empire itself. Diplomacy, governance, public communication, and cultural doctrine all flow through Vox — a house that understands that the story of the Hegemony is as important as its armies or its technology.

**House Name (family name used by children):** Vox

---

## Role & Domain

- **Continental Seat:** [[Voxara Continent]]
- **Capital Megacity:** Vox Imperialis
- **Pillar:** Governance, diplomacy, communications, and cultural influence
- **Genetic Specialization:** House Vox cultivates oratory, social cognition, and ideological fluency in its bloodline — producing nobles who can hold a room, move a population, or dismantle a diplomatic crisis with the right sequence of words

---

## House Head (Patrilium Representative)

**Elia Caerulius Vox Quarta**
Patriarch of House Vox. A former heir candidate, her cognomen *Quarta* marks her as the child of a third concubine. She is the most publicly visible Patriarch in the Hegemony — a deliberate choice. She governs Vox Imperialis, oversees imperial media doctrine, and speaks on behalf of the [[Patrilium]] in all formal public addresses. She is an extraordinary orator and fully aware that her visibility is itself a form of power; people who trust the face of authority rarely question what stands behind it.

---

## Principal Wife (Matrilium Representative)

**Calla Vox**
Principal Wife of House Vox to the current Hegemon and sitting member of the [[Matrilium]]. The council's natural moderator — composed, fair-seeming, and skilled at building consensus between parties who would otherwise never agree. What she advocates for with that consensus, however, is always ultimately aligned with the interests of House Vox.
