## Overview

House Genesis is the third of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], governing [[Genethra Continent]] and sustaining the empire from the ground up. Masters of life itself, Genesis oversees the agricultural megafarms that feed the Hegemony, the genetic refinement programs that shape its bloodlines, and the biological sciences that define what it means to be Helian.

**House Name (family name used by children):** Genesis

---

## Role & Domain

- **Continental Seat:** [[Genethra Continent]]
- **Capital Megacity:** Nova Genesis
- **Pillar:** Agriculture, genetics, and biological sciences
- **Genetic Specialization:** House Genesis cultivates biological intuition, patience, and systems-level thinking in its bloodline — producing nobles who think in generations rather than years, and who regard every living thing as something to be understood, optimized, and preserved

---

## House Head (Patrilium Representative)

**Orin Caerulius Genesis Quinta**
Patriarch of House Genesis. A former heir candidate, his cognomen *Quinta* marks him as the child of a fourth concubine. He is the quietest power on the [[Patrilium]] — measured, long-thinking, and deeply aware that whoever controls the food supply and the bloodline sciences controls the Hegemony's future more surely than any fleet. He oversees Nova Genesis and its vast network of bio-industrial facilities with the patience of a man who has never once needed to hurry.

---

## Principal Wife (Matrilium Representative)

**Senna Genesis**
Principal Wife of House Genesis to the current Hegemon and sitting member of the [[Matrilium]]. A trained biological scientist in her own right — unusual among principal wives — she brings a clinical precision to court politics that makes her colleagues quietly uncomfortable. She is the foremost voice on matters of succession biology and [[Helion Nodes]] research within the Matrilium.
