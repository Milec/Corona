## Overview

House Aegis is the first of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], governing [[Aegyrium Continent]] and serving as the defensive spine of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. Where [[House Bellator]] strikes outward, Aegis holds the line — manning fortress-cities, orbital shield arrays, and strategic command networks that protect the Hegemony from both external invasion and internal collapse.

**House Name (family name used by children):** Aegis

---

## Role & Domain

- **Continental Seat:** [[Aegyrium Continent]]
- **Capital Megacity:** Aegyrion Prime
- **Pillar:** Defensive command, logistics, and strategic coordination
- **Genetic Specialization:** House Aegis cultivates tactical acuity, heightened threat assessment, and exceptional endurance in its bloodline — producing nobles famed as tacticians, admirals, and guardians of imperial stability

---

## House Head (Patrilium Representative)

**Varro Caerulius Aegis Tertius**
Patriarch of House Aegis. A former heir candidate, his cognomen *Tertius* marks him as the child of a second concubine in his cycle. His bearing is deliberate and unshakeable; he speaks rarely in the [[Patrilium]] but is listened to absolutely when he does. He governs Aegyrion Prime with the same philosophy he applies to warfare — overwhelming preparation, minimal exposure, and no unnecessary movement.

---

## Principal Wife (Matrilium Representative)

**Valeria Aegis**
Principal Wife of House Aegis to the current Hegemon and sitting member of the [[Matrilium]]. She is regarded as one of the most tactically minded voices on the council — a woman who approaches court politics the same way her house approaches war: with overwhelming preparation and no visible weakness.
