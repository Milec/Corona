## Overview

House Aurum is the second of [[The Seven Noble Dynasties]], governing [[Aurion Continent]] and controlling the economic lifeblood of the [[Helios Hegemony]]. Gold in name and in nature, Aurum turns prosperity into power — wielding finance, energy production, and intercontinental trade networks as instruments of political dominance.

**House Name (family name used by children):** Aurum

---

## Role & Domain

- **Continental Seat:** [[Aurion Continent]]
- **Capital Megacity:** Sol Aurelia
- **Pillar:** Wealth, trade, and energy production
- **Genetic Specialization:** House Aurum cultivates economic intuition, persuasive capability, and long-horizon strategic thinking — producing nobles who navigate markets and courts with equal ease, and who understand that controlling resources is the quietest form of conquest

---

## House Head (Patrilium Representative)

**Cassia Caerulius Aurum Sept**
Patriarch of House Aurum. A former heir candidate, her cognomen *Sept* marks her as the child of a sixth concubine — the least expected rank from which to ascend to a Patriarchal seat, a fact she has leveraged as a personal myth for her entire political career. She controls Sol Aurelia's energy distribution agreements and has never needed to raise her voice to be obeyed.

---

## Principal Wife (Matrilium Representative)

**Lyris Aurum**
Principal Wife of House Aurum to the current Hegemon and sitting member of the [[Matrilium]]. She is the most publicly celebrated member of the council — luminous, socially impeccable, and far more dangerous than she appears. Her influence over court culture and patronage networks is rivaled by none.
