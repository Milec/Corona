## Overview

Solas is the reigning Sovereign of [[The Eclipse Knights]] — supreme commander of the order's doctrine, its strategy, and every decision that carries weight. A Sovereign holds the seat until death, or until challenged and defeated by a member of Blade rank or above. That Solas has held it unchallenged says everything the order needs said about Solas's strength.

---

## The Sovereign

The Eclipse Knights believe that strength is the only truth that cannot be falsified — and their Sovereign is the living argument for it. No one reaches the seat, or keeps it, on lineage or politics; the order has neither. Solas commands because, in an order that respects nothing else, no one has been able to prove otherwise.

Solas embodies the creed to its coldest edge. It was Solas who dispatched [[Marcus]] on his Proving, into Imperial space, to locate and defeat a designated target — and Solas who, when the Squire was captured on the way out, judged a rescue unsurvivable and let him remain in an Imperial cell.

---

## Doctrine

The order thinks in generations, and so does its Sovereign. The liberation of [[Helios Prime]] — the exposure of the nobility's manufactured powers, the return of true [[Solarian]] ability to the Helian people — is the reason the order exists. But it is a goal to be *reached*, not a banner to spend the order's few lives on. Solas will not trade the future of the order for a single Blade, however rare.

Whether that calculation is wisdom or a failure of nerve is a matter Marcus may one day have his own opinion about. Should he ever return strong enough to force the question, the order's own law provides the answer: a Sovereign serves only until a stronger hand takes the seat.
